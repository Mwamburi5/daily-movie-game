3 · Connections tiles — from portrait slabs to punched ticket stubs

Shipped tiles are 87×108 portrait rectangles — taller than wide, title floating in blank paper, ~250px of dead cream under the grid. Proposal: landscape 62px stubs with a perforation row and side notches (identical on every tile — nothing leaks the group), grid tightens by ~180px so the board + buttons sit together. Selection stays amber. Solved bands unchanged.

4 · Menu mode cards — "borders do the work"

The token sheet's own rule. Shipped menu cards are borderless rounded rects with soft shadows — the one place the app drops the Stub's signature move. Proposal: 2px navy frame + punched side notches (the 7d score-stub treatment), practice pills go navy-outline instead of untokened grey, and the difficulty pill text never wraps.
